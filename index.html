THE CAT AND THE FISH

The big cat fell into the river and I saw a kitten riding a giant fish. The kitten and I locked eyes. It said "meow."


The little kitty gets ready to pounce onto the nearby pile of fish. The fish cried and asked for merci. The cat, however, was ruthless and ate it!


"You feral beast how dare you gnaw on Graham," I screamed and chased the cat far away. The poor cat fell off the cliff and turned into a cat.rar
